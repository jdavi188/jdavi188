Milestone 1


Topic of Choice: The Development Process of 35mm Film + Prints

What is the purpose of the site?
To inform photographers that are interested in 35mm film, how to develop film and prints. This website will also include print manipulation techniques to get 'different' and 'cool' prints.

What do you want the web site to accomplish?
I want this site to be a learning opportunity and digital source for people who want to learn an old, yet remarkably interesting skill.

What opportunity, problem, or issue is your site addressing?
With DSLRs cameras and digital photography overtaking darkroom photography, this once popular task has become rare. I want to inform people about where and how photography started.

Who is your intended audience? Be specific.
Experienced digital photographers, unexperienced photographers, and just about any artist willing to learn a classic skill.

What type of content might be included in your site? Be specific.
How to tutorials on how to develop film and prints, how to manipulate them, and different process techniques. Might include a breif history.

Two related sources.
B & H Photography is a website with equipment and tutorials. Similar but dedicated to everything photography.
Ilford Photo is a company that makes quality film, photopaper and darkroom supplies. They also have tutorials on their site on how to use their products.
